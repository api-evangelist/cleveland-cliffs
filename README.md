# Cleveland-Cliffs (cleveland-cliffs)

Cleveland-Cliffs is the largest flat-rolled steel producer in North America and a major supplier of iron ore pellets. The company operates an integrated steel business including mining, steelmaking, and downstream finishing facilities. Cleveland-Cliffs serves a diverse range of markets including automotive, infrastructure, and manufacturing.
